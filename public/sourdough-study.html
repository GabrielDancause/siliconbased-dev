Methodology

We extracted structured JSON-LD data from each recipe page when available. When structured data was missing or incomplete, we parsed the raw HTML to calculate hydration from ingredient weights (or converted cups to grams at standard ratios: 1 cup flour = 120g, 1 cup water = 240g). Missing data points (e.g., recipes that do not list an oven temperature or bake time) were excluded from those specific averages.
