Methodology

We selected 100 popular websites across 7 major industries (Tech, Media, Ecommerce, Finance, Social, Travel, and Gaming). For each site, we performed an automated GET request to their primary domain.

We followed up to 3 redirects and measured the total raw byte size of the initial HTML response body. This study focuses exclusively on the initial document weight (HTML + inline CSS/JS) and does not include external subresources like images, external scripts, or external stylesheets.
