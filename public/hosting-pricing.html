Methodology

Data was collected directly from official provider pricing pages in 2026. Free tier limits and paid tier starting prices were extracted. Where exact numbers for certain limits (like edge locations or function execution times) were not transparently listed, they were marked as null to prioritize accurate data over estimates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which cloud provider has the best free tier?

Cloudflare Pages generally offers the best free tier for static hosting due to unmetered bandwidth. Vercel and Netlify offer excellent developer experiences but have stricter limits (typically 100GB/mo).

How much does AWS bandwidth cost?

AWS charges roughly $0.09 to $0.15 per GB for outbound data transfer depending on the service (like CloudFront or EC2) and region.

Do Vercel and Netlify charge for bandwidth?

Yes. While both provide a generous free allowance, their overage rates for bandwidth are substantially higher than underlying infrastructure providers like AWS or DigitalOcean.

Are custom domains free on hosting platforms?

Yes. The standard across almost all platforms (Vercel, Netlify, Cloudflare, Fly.io, etc.) is to include custom domain support even on free tiers.

Which platform is cheapest for edge functions?

Cloudflare Workers offers a very competitive pricing model with a generous free tier (100k requests/day) and low costs at scale compared to traditional serverless offerings.
