What is a URL Slug?

A URL slug is the exact part of a URL that identifies a specific page on a website in a readable format. For example, in https://example.com/blog/what-is-a-url-slug, the slug is what-is-a-url-slug.

Slugs are typically created by taking a title, converting it to lowercase, removing special characters, and replacing spaces with hyphens. This makes the URL easier for both humans and search engines to read and understand.

Why are SEO-friendly Slugs Important?

Using clean, descriptive slugs is a best practice for Search Engine Optimization (SEO). Good slugs:

  • Improve User Experience: Users can predict the content of a page just by looking at the URL.
  • Help Search Engines: Search engines use the words in the slug to understand what the page is about.
  • Are Easier to Share: Clean URLs look more trustworthy when shared on social media or in emails compared to long, unreadable URLs with parameters.
